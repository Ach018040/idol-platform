# Idol Pitch Deck Skill

Generate investor decks, product proposals, virtual exhibition proposals, AI idol interaction proposals, and publishing concepts.

## Required Inputs

- Audience: investor, partner, organizer, media, internal product
- Product thesis
- Market evidence
- Roadmap
- Monetization and risk

## Output Contract

- HTML deck
- Speaker notes
- Export plan for PDF/PPTX
- Source appendix

## Checklist

- Start with the market wedge.
- Show why idol-platform is data infrastructure, not only a fan site.
- Keep claims tied to platform data or external source notes.
- Do not imply unauthorized commercial endorsement by a real idol.
- Clearly label simulated examples, generated visuals, and speculative projections.
- Preserve prompt and timestamp in the appendix or metadata.
