# Idol Virtual Exhibition Skill

Generate a virtual exhibition page for idol market data, group introductions, member walls, event timelines, cheki economics, fan culture observations, and AI character interaction showcases.

## Exhibition Zones

- Market data entrance
- Group introduction
- Member data wall
- Event timeline
- Merch and cheki economy model
- Fan culture observation
- AI interactive character showcase

## Checklist

- Make navigation clear and exhibit-like.
- Use source badges for ranking and market data.
- Keep each zone scannable as a standalone panel.
- Mark AI-generated idol depictions clearly.
- Do not create unauthorized face, voice, or identity simulation.
- Avoid misleading political or commercial persuasion.
