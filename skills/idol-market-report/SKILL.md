# Idol Market Report Skill

Generate weekly market reports, cheki merch analysis, fan culture observations, and social activity summaries.

## Required Inputs

- Ranking snapshot
- Formula version
- Event calendar summary
- Social activity freshness and confidence
- Merch or fan behavior notes

## Output Contract

- Markdown report
- HTML report artifact
- Executive summary
- Evidence/source table

## Checklist

- Explain formula limits plainly.
- Distinguish observed data from inferred analysis.
- Include next actions for platform operators.
- Keep prompts, data snapshots, provider, and generation time for audit.
- Do not present generated claims as official statements by idols or agencies.
- Avoid malicious framing or humiliating presentation of real people.
